ROVER uptime status

Graphs represent 24 hours of data. Right edge of the graph is the newest data. Left edge of the graph is data from 24 hours ago.

Green means the connection was working. Numbers are hours. Darkened section represents the work day (8:00 - 16:30) Red bars mean the device did not respond. Occasional single red bars are normal.

Dates are shown in Universal Time (UTC).
