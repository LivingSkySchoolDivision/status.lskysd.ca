School Division Primary Internet Connection

The gauge below indicates how much traffic is currently flowing through the division's primary internet connection (as an average from the last 5 minutes).

If the Internet connection is down for any reason, this will read "<1" indicating that no traffic is flowing. If this gauge is filled, or almost filled, Internet access will appear to be slow due to the amount of traffic.

All schools and sites share this connection, regardless of how fast the connection is at their location.

Individual Sites

Graphs below represent the last 6 hours of traffic. Throughput is on the left, ping latency is on the right. The right edge of the graph is "now".

Outages can be identified by tall red bars appearing on the ping latency graph (on the right), indicating that the site did not respond to a ping. Some red bars are normal, but multiple red bars in a row (or wider red bars) may indicate problems. Orange or Yellow bars in the ping graph indicate network slowness, usually due to the connection running at or near capacity.
