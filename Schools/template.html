Primary Living Sky Internet connection

School's networks are all connected together, and they all meet in the Living Sky Datacenter. The Internet connection at a given school don't connect directly out to the internet, it has to travel through our network first.

This means that there can be two bottlenecks between your computer at the school and the Internet; The connection between the school and our network, and the connection between our network and the Internet.

All schools share this connection, so if one school is using the Internet heavily, it can potentially slow the connection down at all of the other schools.

Ping Latency

Ping latency represents the time it takes a packet to travel from one destination to another (in milliseconds). In this case, the number below represents the time it takes data to travel from our core network to the server at this school. Higher numbers mean a slower response time.

In most cases, this number should be between 0ms and 50ms.

Ping health rating

Health rating of 100% means that there have been no failures in the last two attempts to get data.

Health rating of 50% means that in the last 2 attempts to get data, there was one failure. This is usually a false positive, as servers will not always responsd to a ping fast enough.

Health rating of 0% means that in the last 2 attempts to get data, both attempts failed. This usually means that the site is offline.
